Welcome to CKD Prediction

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is a serious condition where the kidneys are damaged and can't filter blood the way they should. This can cause waste to build up in your body and lead to other health problems like heart disease, stroke, and early death. Early detection and treatment can help prevent the progression of kidney disease and improve your quality of life.

Facts About Kidney Disease

1. CKD often has no symptoms in its early stages and can go unnoticed until it is quite advanced.
2. High blood pressure and diabetes are the two main causes of CKD.
3. Early detection can help manage the disease and prevent serious complications.
4. A healthy diet and lifestyle can significantly reduce the risk of developing CKD.
5. Regular check-ups with your doctor are crucial for early diagnosis and management of CKD.
